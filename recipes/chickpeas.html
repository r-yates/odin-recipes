Chickpeas in air fryer

Description

Crispy chickpeas without the mess and effort of a conventional oven or deep fryer!

Ingredients

  • Canned garbanzo beans
  • 1/4 tbsp paprika
  • 1/4 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tbsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tbsp Tabasco hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees.
  2. Drain and rinse chickpeas (no need to dry).
  3. Mix EVOO and dry ingredients in bowl.
  4. Toss rinsed chickpeas in solution.
  5. Add lemon juice and Tabasco to tossed chickpeas and toss again.
  6. Dump chickpeas into air fryer and cook for 12-14 minutes, or until crispy.
