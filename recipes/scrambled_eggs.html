Scrambled eggs

Description

Some hot ass scrambled eggs, discovered by yours truly

Ingredients

  • 4 large white eggs
  • 200ml liquid egg whites
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • (optional) Tabasco hotsauce

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs until light and fluffy; add egg whites and whisk again until it returns to that same fluffy, homogenous consistency.
  2. Heat EVOO in saaucepan at medium-high to high heat.
  3. Cook spinach until slightly wilted.
  4. Pour egg solution into saucepan, reduce heat to medium to medium-high.
  5. Add butter to saucepan.
  6. Cook until sufficiently scrambled. Leave leeway for aftercooking.
  7. Remove pan from heat, add salt and Tabasco if you desire.
  8. Plate and enjoy.
